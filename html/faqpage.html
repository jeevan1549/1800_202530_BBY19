How do I create a task?

To create a task, press the big plus button at the bottom of your screen in the task list page. It will redirect you to the task creation page.

How do I check my leaderboard status?

To check your leaderboard status go into the podium page. If you are in the top three you should pop up here! If not click the leaderboard button at the top of the page, and it will show you the list of your friends, and their status (including yours).

How do I find my archived tasks?

On the task list page, click the archive icon in the top right corner. This will take you to your archived tasks. Here you can review or delete your tasks. (Once a task is deleted from here it is gone forever)

How do I interact with my pet?

There are 3 ways of interacting with your pet: playing, petting and feeding. To feed your pet you must buy food from the store, and then press the feed button underneath your pet. To play with your pet, you must buy a toy from the store, then press the play button under your pet. Petting your pet is free! Just press the pet button.
